Recipe for proja

Description

Proja is a traditional Serbian cornbread made with a combination of cornmeal, salt, and water.

As soon as milk, beaten eggs, and butter or oil are added to this peasant dish, it's no longer a cornbread but a savory pie called projara, projanica, razlevak, or muruzara, depending on which part of Serbia you find yourself in.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of corn flour
  • 1 cup of white flour
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 glass of carbonated water
  • 1 glass of sour milk or yogurt
  • 3 eggs
  • 500 g of full-fat cheese
  • 1 baking powder
  • a bit of salt

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients and pour them into a Teflon tray (previously slightly greased with oil).
  2. Bake at 220 degrees until golden brown (about half an hour).
