Recipe for razljevak

Description

Recently, I watched a program on television about food in Herzegovina, which included a presnac. I didn't write down the ingredients and quantities, because when I realized that it could be a very good pie as a savory appetizer or dessert, the ingredients disappeared from the screen.

TSo I started making it from memory, and I tried the quantity. Already for the first time I got an excellent taste. And then I added a little cream and it really turned out to be a wonderful salty pie (can be without cream).

Ingredients

  • 2 large cups (for white coffee) of flour
  • 1.5 cups of milk
  • Salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 0.5 dl of oil or fat

Steps

  1. Grease the pan with grease and heat it in the oven at 220 degrees.
  2. Beat eggs, milk and flour, salt, the mixture should be thicker than for pancakes.
  3. Pour over the hot fat in the pan. Bake until the dough is golden brown.
  4. Put the dough into cubes and pour over the dressing.
  5. Return to the oven and bake for another 5-10 minutes.
  6. You can also add 200 g of fresh cream to the sour cream and sour milk.
